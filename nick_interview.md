### Пример поста в стиле который нравится Нику (личный, неформальный, с самоиронией)

Lol this is a first for me. I had some long term US stocks sitting in my portfolio for stability. They barely moved for months, just sat there doing nothing while everything else was happening.
Then another opportunity came up last week and I thought okay, time to finally reshuffle. Sold the bonds, moved the cash into a broader equity position. Made the trades yesterday morning and closed my laptop feeling pretty good about the rebalance.
About an hour later Trump posts on Truth Social about pausing strikes on Iran and "productive talks" toward a deal. Markets absolutely ripped. Dow jumped 600+ points, S&P up over 1%, something like $1.7 trillion added to stocks in minutes. The positions I had just bought were suddenly way up.
I have accidentally become a genius market timer. Please do not ask me for investment advice because I have absolutely no idea what I'm doing and this will never happen again.

Key style notes from this example:
- Opens with "Lol" or casual hook, not a thesis
- Specific details (Dow 600+, $1.7T, "closed my laptop feeling pretty good")
- No buzzwords, no lessons, no framework
- Punchy self-deprecating last line is the whole point
- Reads like something you'd text a friend, not a LinkedIn post
